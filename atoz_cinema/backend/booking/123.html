Aryan Agnihotri is a robotics engineer whose family wants him to get married, but he shies away from the same and goes to the USA to work with his aunt Urmila who runs a robotics company. In Urmila's absence, her manager Sifra is given the responsibility to look after Aryan. They both get close to each other during the short period and develop feelings.
